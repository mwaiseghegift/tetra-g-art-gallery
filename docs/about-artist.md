## 1. My Artistic Journey Page

**My Artistic Journey**

*From pencil sketches to large-scale recycled art installations*

### 1. Artistic Beginnings in Mombasa (Early Years)

- Early life in Mombasa
- Started drawing in school
- Developed a passion for creativity and storytelling through art
- Inspired by the coastal environment and everyday life experiences

#### 2. Academic Foundations (2018-Present)

- Studied at high school level, with Katumanga High School referenced visually
- Artistic skills recognized by peers and teachers
- Worked on murals and school art projects
- Learned how art can transform spaces and environments
- Began exploring environmental and social themes in art

#### 3. Breakthrough with Recycled Materials (2023)

- Began creating artwork using recycled jeans and other materials
- Inspired by sustainability and environmental awareness
- Made a major shift toward eco-art installations
- Focused on transforming waste into meaningful visual art
- Example project: *Nelson Mandela / Dedan Kimathi portrait works*
- Repurposed over 400-700 pieces of denim/jeans, with the visible statistic expanded later

#### 4. Community Collaboration (2023-2024)

- Worked with children in community and home programs
- Led creative workshops and environmental awareness activities
- Created collaborative art using recycled bottle tops
- Developed a large-scale community engagement project
- Collected over 6,732 bottle tops, shown on the page card

#### 5. Shadows of Legacy (2025)

- Tribute-based art installation
- Uses timber offcuts and recycled materials
- Focuses on environmental awareness and storytelling
- Symbolically represents historical figures, including Wangari Maathai
- Explores sustainability through sculpture and mixed media

#### 6. The Journey Continues

- Artistic evolution is ongoing
- Focus on larger installations and sustainable materials
- Blends culture, heritage, and environmental storytelling
- Future direction includes innovation and expanded installations
